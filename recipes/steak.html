Steak

Description

This steak is a really yummy medium rare steak cooked with some tomatoes, herbs, and butter.

Meal Description

  • 6oz Steak
  • Butter
  • Tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Cook steak until brown crust
  2. Flip every 2 minutes
  3. Coat with butter
  4. Cook until medium rare, check online for specific temperatures
