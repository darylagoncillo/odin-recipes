Chicken

Description

This meal is a really yummy chicken cooked with some tomatoes, herbs, and butter.

Meal Description

  • 6oz Chicken
  • Butter
  • Tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Cook chicken until brown crust
  2. Flip every 2 minutes
  3. Coat with butter
  4. Cook until super well-- or else you'll get sick! Check online for specific temperatures
